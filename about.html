About Me

Piotr Stachaczynski

Backend Developer & AI Enthusiast

Hello there! I'm Piotr, a software developer with a passion for backend systems, AI, and occasionally dabbling in game development when time permits.

I currently work on building robust backend solutions and exploring the fascinating world of artificial intelligence. When I'm not writing code for work, I'm probably tinkering with personal projects or learning about new technologies.

I'm the owner and maintainer of MaIN.NET, an open-source project designed for integrating Large Language Models (LLMs) with ease in the .NET ecosystem.

Game development has been my childhood dream, and while I haven't had as much time to dedicate to it as I'd like, I'm hoping to do more in this space. I believe the intersection of AI and game development offers particularly exciting possibilities.
